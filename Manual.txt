Slide into Highschool Manual

Main method in Driver class
Description: A interactive game for elementary/middle school students to show them what it's like to be a highschooler

Level 1, Deficiencies game:
- Description: learn about different object you'll need in high school
- Click on objects to learn about them
- Double click to move on or exit

Level 2, Maze game:
- Description: make it to the end of the maze by overcoming obstacles along the way
- move with 'w' 'a' 's' 'd'

Level 3, Maze game:
- Description: complete a day in high school by playing the minigames in different rooms around the school
- move with 'w' 'a' 's' 'd'
- click the green button on the bottom left to play the games
